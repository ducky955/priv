Tools

Choosing the right tools is critical to maintaining online anonymity. Here, we categorize tools into two categories: Open Source (FOSS) and Paid Services.

Tool Categories

🌐 Browsers & Operating Systems

  • Tor Browser: The gold standard for anonymous web browsing.
  • Tails OS: A live operating system that runs from a USB stick and routes all traffic through Tor.
  • Qubes OS: A security-focused operating system that uses virtualization to isolate different activities.

🛡️ VPNs & Proxies

  • Mullvad VPN: A privacy-focused VPN that doesn't require an email address or personal details to register.
  • ProtonVPN: A reputable VPN service based in Switzerland with a strong focus on security.

💬 Encrypted Communication

  • Signal: End-to-end encrypted messaging.
  • Session: An onion-routed, metadata-free messenger that does not require phone numbers.
