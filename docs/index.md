# Priv - Online Anonymity Guide

Welcome to **Priv**, your comprehensive resource for learning about online anonymity and finding the best tools to protect your digital footprint.

## What is Online Anonymity?

Online anonymity is the ability to interact on the internet without revealing your real-world identity. Unlike privacy, which is about controlling what information others can see about you, anonymity is about concealing *who* you are entirely.
