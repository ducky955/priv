# News Overview

## Recent Developments

This page tracks important news and developments in the privacy and anonymity space.

### Categories

#### 🔒 Security Audits
Independent security audits of privacy tools help identify vulnerabilities and validate security claims. Regular audits are a sign of a trustworthy tool.

#### ⚠️ Vulnerabilities  
Zero-day exploits, browser vulnerabilities, and configuration leaks are critical security issues that can compromise anonymity. Staying informed allows you to respond quickly.

#### ⚖️ Regulatory News
Governments worldwide are implementing policies that affect encryption, privacy, and surveillance. Understanding these developments helps you adapt your practices.

#### 📰 Industry News
New tools, protocol improvements, and community developments shape the privacy landscape.
